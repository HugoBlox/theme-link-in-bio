## FAQ

<details>
<summary><b>Do I need to know Hugo?</b></summary>
No. Edit visually with Ownable CMS or write Markdown. Hugo is the build engine — you don't need to touch it.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How is this different from Linktree?</b></summary>
You own the page, host it for free on your own domain, and pay nothing — ever. No paid tier to unlock analytics, custom branding, or more links.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I use my own domain?</b></summary>
Yes. Point any custom domain at your free hosting provider. No paid upgrade required.
</details>
